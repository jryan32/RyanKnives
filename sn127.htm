NOTE: This was the last knife Greig was working on before his death.
As you can see from the photo, the knife appears to be finished. He never got around to etching a serial number or carving a sheath for it but it's still a beautiful knife!
Serial Number: 127
Type: Drop Point
Construction: Full Tang, 9 3/4" Overall Length
Steel: 1/4" ATS-34
Blade: 4 1/2" Mirror Polished with Decorative Filework
Handle: Walnut (we think) with White Spacers, and Brass Pins
Guard: Brass
Sheath: none
