Welcome to our website...and thanks for visiting.

This site was created to allow my Dad (Greig) an opportunity to pursue his art of knife making while at the same time being able to recoup some of his costs by occasionally offering knives for auction via eBay. Unfortunately, in Sept 2001 he suffered a heart attack and passed away...so Ryan Knives will no longer exist except in the hearts of his family and all who had the opportunity to own one of his works of art.

In Jan 2003 we sold the last remaining knife that we had made available from his collection.

Please take a few minutes to read about Dad on the About Maker page and review samples of his work on the Knives page.

If you have any questions or comments on his work, please don't hesitate to email and I'll attempt to answer as best I can...Thanks...JR
